Tales in GloryLand once told stories of Mythical Creatures and "Gods". But, one day, they vanished... Leaving No Trace behind.


Worshippers of these Mythical beast gave birth to five families: The MorningStars, The Luciars, The Flamingo, The Astas and the Dawns. Born of different beliefs and blood lines, these families were born of rivalry.


As 5 villages began to grow into towns and eventually kingdoms, the tension between them worsened. One day, following the discovery of $Gold and $Glory in the lands, conflict broke out.


Greatest heroes gathered within their corresponding Kingdoms. Lords who voiced themselves in council and noble Knights who slayed enemies fearlessly on the battlefields. Joining forces with each other, they learn to fight alongside comrades who share the same belief.


As the war amongst the Five Kingdoms intensifies, more and more heroes are recruited into each kingdom and the greed for these resources blinds even the wisest of all.


At the same time, archaeologist who thoroughly study the history of glory land begans to slowly uncover the hidden truth behind the disappearance of the Mythical Beasts. Where will this all lead to?
